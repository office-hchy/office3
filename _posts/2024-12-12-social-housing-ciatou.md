### Commissioned by MAYU architects & associates, this social housing project aims to not just accommodate the growing population in Kaohsiung, but to create a sense of place, a community. Therefore, culture and territorial symbols of Kaohsiung (such as the famous 85 skyscraper) are shown in the final video.
