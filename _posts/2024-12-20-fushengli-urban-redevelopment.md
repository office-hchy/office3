### Collaborated with KCAP as their first project in Taipei, this video is part of the feasibility study of redeveloping a neighborhood in central Taipei. The challenge of this project is to convince the residents the development creates a better urban space for the vicinity, while reinsure the city concil it is align with the building code. Hence, the result is an animation of moving diagram superimposed with the architect's sketch.
